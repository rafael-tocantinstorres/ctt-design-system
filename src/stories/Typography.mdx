    <div className="usage-guidelines">
      <div className="guideline-item">
        <h4>Font Loading</h4>
        <p>The ActoCTT font family is loaded with font-display: swap for optimal performance. Always specify fallback fonts in your CSS.</p>
      </div>

      <div className="guideline-item">
        <h4>CSS Custom Properties</h4>
        <p>All typography classes use CSS custom properties (CSS variables) defined in our tokens.css file. This ensures consistency and makes it easy to maintain and update the design system.</p>
      </div>

      <div className="guideline-item">
        <h4>Responsive Design</h4>
        <p>Use the appropriate responsive variants (desktop, tablet, mobile) based on your breakpoints and design requirements. The classes are designed to work with your responsive design strategy.</p>
      </div>

      <div className="guideline-item">
        <h4>Accessibility</h4>
        <p>All typography scales maintain proper contrast ratios and are designed to be readable across different devices and accessibility needs. Font sizes are specified in rem units for better scalability.</p>
      </div>

      <div className="guideline-item">
        <h4>Hierarchy</h4>
        <p>Use typography consistently to create clear information hierarchy. Display text for hero content, titles for section headers, body text for content, and labels for UI elements.</p>
      </div>
    </div>
